Tuples are number of values seperated by commas. Elements can be of different kinds.

{% highlight python %}
In [1]: m = 12,67,'ping','ding'

In [2]: m
Out[2]: (12, 67, 'ping', 'ding')

In [3]: n=90,100

In [4]: n
Out[4]: (90, 100)

In [5]: new_tuple=m,n

In [6]: new_tuple
Out[6]: ((12, 67, 'ping', 'ding'), (90, 100))
{% endhighlight %}
